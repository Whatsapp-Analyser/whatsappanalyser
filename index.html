Whatsapp Analyser

An analysis of all the messages that been sent in your group chat

How it works

1. Go to the group chat settings.
2. Hit 'Export Chat'.
3. Save the chat on our device.
4. Upload the txt-file above.

What happens to my data?

Nothing! Your txt-file is uploaded in your browser and is not send to our servers. Making Whatsapp Analyser the only analyser that has absolutely no idea what you and your friends talk about in your chats.

Don't believe us? Good. We love that you care about your data, but we are legit. See the code at our github.
